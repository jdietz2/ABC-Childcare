Our Programs

Here at ABC, we offer childcare and classes from ages 0-6. With a child-to-staff ratio of 3-to-1, your little treasure gets plenty of TLC. Parents must provide disposable diapers, food, and bottles for the day. Staff members are able to follow each child's daily schedule. Take a look below and see what classes we offer.

Nursery (ages 0-1)

With a child-to-staff ratio of 3-to-1, your little treasure gets plenty of TLC. Parents must provide disposable diapers, food, and bottles for the day. Staff members are able to follow each child's daily schedule.

Toddler Daycare (ages 2-3)

For your toddler, we offer enriching activities that build motor skills and encourage creativity. A 5-to-1 child-to-staff ratio ensures personal attention for your budding artist or athlete. Naptime, outside time, a hot lunch, and two snacks are provided.

Daycare (age 4)

Socially and developmentally, preschoolers thrive in music, art, and outdoor play programs geared toward their emerging interests. The child-to-staff ratio is 8-to-1. Quiet time with a video, outside time, a hot lunch, and two snacks are provided.

Pre-Kindergarten (age 5)

Socially and developmentally, preschoolers thrive in music, art, and outdoor play programs geared toward their emerging interests. The child-to-staff ratio is 8-to-1. Quiet time with a video, outside time, a hot lunch, and two snacks are provided.

Kindergarten (age 6)

This class focuses more on education and learning. The classroom environment functions more like a typical kindergarten class with recess, lunch, and learning times.
